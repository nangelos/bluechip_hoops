Blue Chip Hoops

What is Blue Chip Hoops?

Blue Chip Hoops was built to help baseketball coaches manage their programs better. We believe that coaches should use technology to keep up with recruits, current players, other coaches, and events in one simple place.

What Can Blue Chip Hoops Do For You?

Blue Chips Hoops can help you manage contact with recruits, send automated news alerts about key recruits, monitor social media, compile schedules of upcoming events, and even boost your social media profile.
